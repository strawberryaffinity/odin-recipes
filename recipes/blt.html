BLT Recipe

How to Make a BLT Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread (2 slices)
  • Tomato Slice
  • Lettuce
  • Bacon

Instructions

  1. Place one slice of bread on a plate
  2. Place bacon on the slice of bread
  3. Place tomato on the bacon
  4. Place lettuce on the tomato
  5. Place one slice of bread on the lettuce
