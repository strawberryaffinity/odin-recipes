Mac and Cheese Recipe

How to Make Mac and Cheese

Description

An easy and yummy macaroni and cheese recipe! Serves four.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups milk
  • 1 lb elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, boil milk
  2. Add pasta and stir for about 10 minutes until cooked
  3. Turn off heat, then add cheese until melted and pasta is evenly coated
  4. Enjoy!
